Brief History

Ilukat Networks Limited (ILNET) is a multi-functional Business Solution Company formed in 2017 by Ilukat Musa, the company is mainly dealing in Telecommunications, Electrical, IT solutions and Civil works. The company is fully equipped with technical graduates and experienced engineers and other qualified staff in different fields. ILNET initially started by providing Telecom field engineering services for MTN and Airtel mainly on their BTS under QTE, ETEL as the Main contractors but over the years, our service portfolio has grown to IT, Electrical, Civil works and Training of experts in the named services
